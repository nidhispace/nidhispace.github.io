VOCs

Volatile Organic Compounds

Hover to reveal key facts
Anthropogenic & Natural

Emitted from paints, vehicles, industry, and also vegetation. React with NOx to form ground-level ozone.

Example: Benzene, Toluene, Formaldehyde

BVOCs

Biogenic Volatile Organic Compounds

Hover to explore
Ecosystem's Breath

Released by plants, forests, and soil. Isoprene & monoterpenes dominate. Influence aerosol formation.

~90% of global VOC emissions are biogenic

Key Anthropogenic Sources
  • Vehicle exhaust & fuel evaporation
  • Paints, solvents, adhesives
  • Industrial processes & refineries
Atmospheric Fate

VOCs + OH radicals / NOx → ozone & secondary organic aerosols (SOA). Lifetimes range from hours to months.
