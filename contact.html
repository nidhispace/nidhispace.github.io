1871
German Empire Established
The German Empire was proclaimed in the Hall of Mirrors at the Palace of Versailles following the Franco-Prussian War.
1914-1918
World War I
Germany was a central power in World War I, which resulted in a major shift in European politics and the abdication of Kaiser Wilhelm II.
1933
Nazi Party Rises to Power
Adolf Hitler was appointed Chancellor, marking the start of the Third Reich and the lead-up to World War II.
1945
End of World War II
Germany was defeated, resulting in its division into East and West during the Cold War.
1990
German Reunification
East and West Germany reunified after the fall of the Berlin Wall, restoring Germany as a single nation.
Present
Modern Germany
Germany is now a leading European country with strong cultural, economic, and political influence.
